About Us

Biography

Vincent Venus is a Filipino culinary artist who specializes in the field of French cusine. He finds joy in cooking and eating delacacies; it started when his grandmother started operating a France-based restaurant. His restaurant did not come from his own idea, but rather from a recipe book filled with a variety of delicious food. Praised for his delacacies, Vincent was able to establish an official franchise.

Culinary Memoir

With the support of many food lovers, the restaurant rose in popularity and success. La Geule de Venus is famous for it's notable dishes and ambient atmosphere.

Michelin Stars

La Geule de Venus earned its first Meichelin star in the early 20th century. The restaurant's entire menu was treated like one excellent signature dish by crtics.
